Wedge positioning: Context SDK for plant AI

Start as infrastructure for teams already building chatbots. Earn the right to become the governed knowledge layer later.

Narrow first product

1. Instrument

Drop a server SDK into the chat layer. Capture prompts, responses, retrieval hits, feedback, user role, domain, and outcome.

2. Infer

Detect knowledge gaps, repeated failure modes, emerging expert domains, tacit rules, disagreement, and sentiment.

3. Improve

Generate reviewed improvement artifacts: candidate SOP edits, RAG index gaps, SME questions, rules/assertions.

4. Expand

Route approved knowledge into their RAG stack, Confluence/SOP repo, MD1 Knowledge Bank, or future graph layer.

A

Initial buyer promise

Your plant chatbot finally learns from how people use it. We show what it knows, what it misses, who trusts it, and what new knowledge should be codified.

B

Expansion path

Once the SDK reveals valuable tacit knowledge, MD1 becomes the review, governance, graph, lifecycle, and operational memory layer.
