Human review should be visible because it...

  • Makes the extraction claim credible
  • Fits manufacturing governance norms
  • Creates a workflow MD1 can later own deeply
  • Lets the product be bold without publishing risky knowledge automatically

Keep it from feeling slow by...

  • Ranking candidates by confidence and impact
  • Showing source snippets and why the item was extracted
  • Batching similar candidates
  • Exporting approved items directly back into customer tools
