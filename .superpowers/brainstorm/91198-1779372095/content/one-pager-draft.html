MD1 Context SDK: one-pager draft

A concise product narrative plus architecture. This is draft copy we can tighten.

Hero

Turn plant chatbot conversations into operating knowledge.

MD1 Context SDK sits inside your existing AI assistant and mines conversations for the expert know-how, process exceptions, decision logic, and knowledge gaps your RAG system is missing.

Problem

Manufacturing chatbots retrieve what is already documented. But critical plant context shows up in corrections, frustration, local exceptions, repeated questions, and expert follow-up.

RAG retrieves documented knowledge. MD1 mines what conversations reveal is missing, wrong, local, disputed, or known only by experienced people.

Outputs

Knowledge candidates: rules, assertions, SOP deltas, exceptions, thresholds, contradictions.

Chatbot intelligence: usage, sentiment, trust, adoption, unresolved domains.

RAG improvement feed: missing sources, weak chunks, bad citations, stale references.
