Three viable scopes for MD1 Context SDK

Same wedge, different center of gravity. My recommendation is A: Learning Layer, because it combines the strongest buyer pain with the clearest expansion path.

A. Learning Layer

Promise: Make your plant chatbot improve from every conversation.

Outputs: analytics, knowledge candidates, RAG improvement feed.

Expansion: review/governance, MD1 Knowledge Bank, graph.

Recommended

Best balance of boldness, practicality, and differentiation.

B. Agent Analytics for Plants

Promise: See whether your plant AI is working.

Outputs: usage, intent, correction, resolution, sentiment.

Expansion: deeper mining later.

Simplest sale

Easy to understand, but risks looking like a vertical Voker.

C. Knowledge Mining SDK

Promise: Extract hidden operating know-how from chats.

Outputs: candidate rules, exceptions, thresholds, SOP deltas.

Expansion: governance-first MD1 path.

Boldest claim

Differentiated, but may need more proof and review workflow up front.

Competitive whitespace

  • Voker: strong generic agent analytics
  • DeltaMemory/Zep-like products: strong agent memory and graph direction
  • MD1 angle: plant-specific improvement loop plus governed operational knowledge

Do not over-scope v1

  • No replacement chatbot
  • No full document management
  • No autonomous SOP publishing
  • No promise to perfectly infer truth without review
