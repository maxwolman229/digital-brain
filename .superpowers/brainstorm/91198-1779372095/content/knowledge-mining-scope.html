Chosen scope: Knowledge Mining SDK

The sharp wedge: extract hidden operating know-how from existing plant chatbot conversations.

One-pager hierarchy

Turn plant chatbot conversations into operating knowledge.

MD1 Context SDK mines expert know-how, process exceptions, decision logic, and knowledge gaps from your existing AI assistant.

Primary output

Governed knowledge candidates: rules, assertions, SOP deltas, exceptions, thresholds, unresolved expert questions.

Supporting output

Chatbot analytics: domains, users, sentiment, confidence, unanswered clusters, adoption.

Supporting output

RAG improvement feed: missing sources, weak chunks, bad citations, repeated failed queries.

Why this is sharper

  • Differentiates from generic chatbot analytics
  • Connects directly to MD1's core knowledge lifecycle
  • Creates a higher-value artifact than dashboards alone
  • Makes the wedge strategic: the SDK becomes a plant memory intake layer

How to keep it credible

  • Say “candidate” knowledge until reviewed
  • Show confidence, source snippets, and conversation provenance
  • Route sensitive/uncertain items to SMEs
  • Let customers export without adopting all of MD1
