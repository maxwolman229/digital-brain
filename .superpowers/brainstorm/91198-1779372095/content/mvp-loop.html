MVP: lightweight continuous improvement layer

One SDK, three outputs: analytics, knowledge candidates, and RAG improvement signals.

Minimum viable product loop

Input: chat telemetry

Messages, retrieval context, citations, tool calls, feedback, user role, area, session outcome, escalations, edits, and follow-up behavior.

Engine: context mining

Classify domain, detect intent, score success, cluster gaps, extract tacit assertions, identify SME patterns, and flag contradiction.

Analytics

Who asks what, where confidence drops, which domains fail, where adoption is growing.

Candidate queue

Proposed rules, assertions, SOP deltas, unresolved questions, and SME review prompts.

RAG feed

Missing sources, weak chunks, query clusters, stale citations, retrieval failures, and suggested enrichments.

Why this is a good wedge

  • Installs without replacing the chatbot
  • Creates immediate visibility for AI owners
  • Surfaces high-value tacit knowledge early
  • Naturally expands into MD1 governance

Scope guardrails

  • No custom chatbot UI required
  • No full SOP management in v1
  • No autonomous publishing without review
  • No deep MES/ERP integration needed to start
